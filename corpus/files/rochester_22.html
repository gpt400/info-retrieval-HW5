A Campus-wide Approach to Health Promotion

At the UHS Health Promotion Office, we know that promoting positive well-being is fundamental to students academic success. We aim to support the needs of our diverse student population and advance a comprehensive, inclusive, and progressive student wellness model which creates the conditions for all students to flourish.
