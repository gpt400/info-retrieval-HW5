Our cross-discipline areas of distinction

Biomedical and health care innovation

Our biomedical, nursing, and medical research spans an array of illnesses, conditions, and therapies. That research intersects with unique academic experiences and the delivery of cutting-edge clinical care.

Improving health

Just and equitable societies

With our academic strengths focused on policy, theory, and practice in equity and justice, we continue to confront some of most consequential issues facing societal health and well-being.

Tackling inequities

Music excellence and innovation

Were poised to simultaneously embrace the future of music while furthering traditional music performance at the highest levels of excellence.

Reimagining artistry

The digital future

Building on our strengths in fundamental science and engineering, quantitative social science, medicine, and music, the University of Rochester is at the forefront of discovery in the digital age.

Accelerating change

Transformational materials and technologies

Weve long been a world leader in light, optics, imaging, vision, quantum science and engineering, and high-energy-density sciences. New breakthroughs will reshape industries and impact every aspect of our lives.
