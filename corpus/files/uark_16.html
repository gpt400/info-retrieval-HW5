    Majors

    Make every opportunity to consult your academic adviser to help you take advantage of the courses and campus resources that will help you not only graduate on time, but also reach your educational and career goals. Undergraduate students looking for guidance in choosing a major can schedule with a major exploration coach, who can help you make sense of majors and the major selection process.

    The faculty and staff are here to support you as you work to achieve your goals. Ask for help and advice whenever you need it. Remember, the University of Arkansas is committed to your success.
