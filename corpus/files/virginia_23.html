Our Mission

The mission of the University of Virginia Naval ROTC is to develop midshipmen morally, mentally, and physically in order to commission naval officers possessing the highest ideals of service and selflessness. UVA Naval ROTC imbues our midshipmen with the core values of Honor, Courage, and Commitment and with the core attributes of Initiative, Accountability, Integrity, and Toughness that are required of all Navy and Marine Corps officers. UVA NROTC graduates shall possess the professional knowledge, skills, and abilities worthy of the special trust and confidence that will be placed in them upon commissioning.
