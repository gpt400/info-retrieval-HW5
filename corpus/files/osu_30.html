Matching Gifts

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match any charitable contributions made by their employees. If your company is eligible, the impact of your gift to The Ohio State University may be doubled or possibly tripled! Some companies even match gifts made by retirees and/or spouses.

Arranging a Matching Gift

To arrange for a matching gift, submit the request via your employer's online matching gift portal, or complete a hard copy form and mail it to the address below. If you have questions or would like to submit your form electronically, please email MatchingGifts@osu.edu

The Ohio State University Foundation
Attn: Matching Gifts
14 E 15th Ave, Columbus, OH 43201

Note: If you are requesting a match for a gift made to BuckeyeThon, please designate "The Ohio State University Foundation" as the recipient organization.
