Safety Resources & Mobile Apps

NUGuardian

A free peer-to-peer mobile app that turns your phone into a personal safety device by letting you ask smartphone contacts to virtually walk with you on or off campus in Evanston.

Download NUGuardian

Safe Ride

A safe and free alternative to walking alone after dark on or near the Evanston campus.

Download Safe Ride

Campus Shuttles

Free shuttles between the Evanston and Chicago campuses that run late into the night.

Shuttle Routes

Safety Escorts Program

A free service to provide a walking escort or to monitor travel on the Evanston or Chicago campuses.
Request a Safety Escort
