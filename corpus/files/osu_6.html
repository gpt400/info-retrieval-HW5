What sets Columbus apart

Columbus is joyful, inclusive, exciting and growing. It has an amazing vibe thats unlike any other city. Discover what makes Columbus so uniqueand so great.

Experience Columbus culture

See what makes this city so special

An annual event celebrating LGBTQIA+ rights, culture and progress, the Columbus Pride parade is colorful, joyous occasion that brings the community together in camaraderie and recognition.

Held by the beautiful downtown riverfront, the Columbus Arts Festival is an amazingand freeway to soak in the culture. From local artists to national enthusiasts, theres sure to be something that inspires you.

The Arena District just south of Ohio State's campus is home to sports arenas and music venues. Catch a bite to eat before a live concert or your favorite sports events.

With a passionate and lively fanbase, a Columbus Crew soccer game is something that cant be missed. So suit up in some black and gold, head out the field and get ready for a roaring good time.

Iconic in its beauty, Mirror Lake is an Ohio State staple. From the hustle and bustle of students to the subtle sounds of nature, no trip to Columbus is complete without stopping here.

From award-winning restaurants to breweries galore, there are many culinary treasures to explore. Grab a brew, a bite to eat, ice cream for dessert whatever youre in the mood for!
