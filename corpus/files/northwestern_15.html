Northwestern commits to preventing sexual harassment in higher education

The University joins other major institutions in National Academies' action collaborative. Read more here.

Residential Services announces the expansion of gender-open housing options for all undergraduate students

Learn more about gender-open housing options and review FAQ's on the Residential Services website.

Office of Civil Rights and Title IX Compliance participates in panel event to discuss proposed changes to Title IX regulations

On January 11th, 2019, staff from the Office of Civil Rights and Title IX Compliance participated in a panel event to discuss proposed changes to Title IX regulations. Read more about the event here.

Northwestern’s Annual Report for the Illinois Preventing Sexual Violence in Higher Education Act:

On November 1, 2017, Northwestern submitted its annual report to the state of Illinois providing information on certain reports of sexual violence, domestic violence, dating violence, and stalking brought by or on behalf of students during calendar year 2016. That report can be found here.
