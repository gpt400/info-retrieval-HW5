About the Center for Community Engagement

The Center for Community Engagement offers students volunteer and service-learning opportunities. Programs and services are designed for students, faculty, staff and community partners, helping to connect University resources with community needs. The University also serves the community through programs and resources housed in the historic John S. and Drucie R. Chase Building.
