Leadership boards

Alumni board

The University of Rochester Alumni Board engages all alumni and students in a lifelong connection with one another and with the University. Comprised of individuals from a variety of schools, class years, and backgrounds, this group serves as a voice for the more than 115,000 graduates that make up our global network.

Learn more

Diversity Advisory Council

The Diversity Advisory Council (DAC) is a national volunteer body responsible for providing leadership, guidance, and feedback to the University of Rochester on diversity and inclusion initiatives for its alumni and students.
