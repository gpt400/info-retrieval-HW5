Supporting Wildcats for Life

Arizona Alumni serves more than 300,000 former University of Arizona students, creating a community for Wildcats to remain connected to their alma mater and to each other. Arizona alumni give back to their communities in 50 U.S. states and in more than 150 countries. We foster alumni connections and are the gateway to the university through resources such as alumni chapters and clubs, communications, travel and special events.

Arizona Loyal

Arizona Loyal is a giving program that celebrates the universitys most devoted champions. Anyone who makes a gift of any size to the university for two or more years in a row joins Arizona Loyal.

Now, you can support Arizona Alumni directly or make a gift to support any unit or program of your choice across the university. Renew your giving every year and stay Arizona Loyal!

  • Support impactful programs and initiatives
  • Contribute to student scholarships
  • Fuel groundbreaking research

Your connection to the University of Arizona does not end when you graduate. You are a Wildcat for Life. Our people, traditions and history create extraordinary experiences for Wildcats the world over. 

Find Your Alumni Network

Arizona Alumni networks support the deep and lasting connection we all share with the University of Arizona and help preserve the Wildcat spirit. Join your local network to stay connected to fellow Wildcats and the university.

Join an Alumni Community

Arizona Alumni communities offer Wildcats the opportunity to connect with diverse and like-minded fellow alumni who share cultures and experiences. Alumni Communities provide support for students through scholarships while also celebrating alumni who make a difference in their communities.
