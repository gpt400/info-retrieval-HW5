Applying to BU College of Fine Arts

First Year Applicants

Apply as a first-year student if you are currently in your last year of high school, have completed high school, are a GED recipient, or have completed a high school equivalency exam.

Read More

Early Decision Applicants

Are you absolutely sure that College of Fine Arts is your top choice school? Then, why wait? Explore early decision application to Boston University.

Read More

Transfer Applicants

Whether you are a BU student thinking of transferring into CFA or an undergrad at an external college, we are thrilled you are considering joining our community.

Read More

International Applicants

Boston University defines an international student to be a non-US citizen or non-US Permanent Resident Green Card Holder. Learn more about the process of applying to CFA.
