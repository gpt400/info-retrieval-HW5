Overview of Handbook

The Northwestern University Student Handbook describes the expectations for behavior and conduct in the Northwestern community and outlines the procedures to be followed when these expectations are not met. The Student Handbook includes the Student Code of Conduct, the Academic Integrity Policy, as well as other rules, regulations, and policies governing student life.
