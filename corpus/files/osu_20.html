Together, We Build a Better Tomorrow

The Employee Giving program encourages all Ohio State employees to connect with programs and causes that reflect their passions.

Employee Giving includes all the programs you know, such as Buckeyes for Charity and Team Buckeye, but also provides a pathway for you to discover causes that highlight the extraordinary work happening at Ohio State and in our community.

Buckeyes dont give for the glory. We are champions for the students, researchers, dreamers and doers who will change the world in ways that have not yet been imagined. Employee Giving provides for the future of Ohio State through our work and through our support of causes which fuel our passions.

We create a circle of Buckeyes who are committed to making the world better. Whether you support a cause related to your career or your personal life, you can participate in a variety of ways, including financial support, education and advocacy.

Make a difference today

Because when we all believe in Ohio State, we are collectively able to promote positive change not only here in Ohio, but across the globe.

When we give, its not about focusing on one moment. Its about the future the long-range impact our generosity will have for decades to come. Were all a part of something bigger than ourselves here at Ohio State.

Your gifts are the ones enabling this university to provide a top-tier education to students, solve complex societal problems and transform the communities around us.

We have the chance to impact the lives of millions through our generosity. And this is how we collectively do it.

Campus Campaign was an annual drive during a dedicated time period each spring during which employees are asked to make a gift to the Ohio State causes that mean something to them. We Inspire is the program replacing Campus Campaign, which encompasses employee giving participation beyond a dedicated time in the spring. Your gift during Day of Giving, BuckeyeThon, Team Buckeye, Class Gift or any College/Unit or cause would all count as employee participation. 

Our goal with this shift is to build a continuous culture of philanthropy and a better employee experience. The issues you are passionate about occur all year-long, and We Inspire is designed to better align with what matters most to you while offering support to those who need it the most.

As a current employee, you have two easy options:

Payroll deduction: sign up today online or using a printed gift form (minimum of $1/pay per fund). Deductions are ongoing gifts through per pay installments and continue until the University is notified otherwise (12 times per year for those paid monthly and 24 times per year for those paid biweekly).

One-time gift: make your gift online or via cash* or check.

*Cash if possible, drop off in person at 2200 Olentangy River Road with your name, address, and fund designation. Please do not send cash through the mail.

One-time payroll deductions cannot be processed. Changes made to payroll deductions will take effect the next possible pay period. Payroll deductions are set up as continuous until further notice. However, with the new implementation of Workday, you now have the opportunity to change your fund designation or amount daily. If you have any questions, please contact Gift and Biographic Records at gifts@osu.edu or 614-292-2141 for assistance.

Yes. Presidents Club is The Ohio State Universitys largest and most prestigious leadership annual giving recognition program. Since 1963, Presidents Club has recognized the most loyal and generous donors to the university. These donors offer their philanthropic and leadership support and share a continuing commitment to this great institution.

Annual recognition is extended to individuals who make non-athletic qualifying gifts of $3,000 or more during the calendar year. For gifts of payroll deduction, recognition is achieved with a monthly contribution of $250 for 12 months (January-December for renewing donors or July-June for new donors). A combination of payroll gifts and cash may also be contributed.

For more information about Presidents Club and the associated benefits, view the website or contact Presidents Club at presidentsclub@osu.edu or 614-292-9550.
