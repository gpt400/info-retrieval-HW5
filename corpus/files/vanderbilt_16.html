Project Safe
Center for Sexual Misconduct Prevention and Response

Quick Exit

24-Hour Crisis/Support Hotline: (615) 322-SAFE (7233)

Project Safe is here for those impacted by gender and sexual harassment, sexual assault, intimate partner violence, stalking and more.

Learn More

Project Safe is located in the Cumberland House on West Side Row.

Project Safe is available 24 hours a day via our crisis/support hotline (615) 322-7233. For non-urgent matters, please call our office line (615) 875-0660 or email projectsafe@vanderbilt.edu. Advocacy appointments are available during our usual business hours of Monday-Friday 8 AM to 5 PM. If you are already connected with a Victim Resource Specialist, please feel free to communicate with them directly via email; otherwise, call or email to request an appointment. Our physical address is 2304H Vanderbilt Place, Nashville, TN 37203.

In An Emergency

The following services are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week:

  • Project Safe Support Hotline: 615-322-SAFE (7233)
  • Vanderbilt University Police: 911 or 615-421-1911
  • Vanderbilt Project Safe staff are available at the Project Safe Center in the Cumberland House on West Side Row, Monday Friday, 8 a.m. 5 p.m.
  • Vanderbilt University Medical Center Emergency Services: 615-322-0160
  • Nashville Sexual Assault Center Hotline: 1-800-879-1999
  • YWCA Crisis and Information Line: 615-242-1199
