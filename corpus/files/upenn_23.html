Brand Standards

The Penn brand is an integral component of the overarching Penn experience.
Our insignia are as central to the University as College Hall, Locust Walk, or Benjamin Franklin.
These symbols play an important role in promoting the University and as such,
it is important that the logo not be physically altered.
