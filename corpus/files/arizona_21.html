Health & Wellness for Students

Health & Wellness for Students

Comprehensive services and support for the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of our students.

CAMPUS HEALTH

Campus Health is a one-stop-shop for health and wellness. From medical, mental health, and wellness services, to events, workshops, and student groups, theyre here to support all students.

Medical & Wellness Services at Campus Health

Primary Care

Doctors, nurse practitioners, nurses, and medical assistants provide quality and student-centered care for routine and immediate health needs.

Primary Care

Same-Day Clinic

Campus Health offers same-day appointments for urgent and unexpected medical needs that come up such as cold/flu symptoms, stitches, IV therapy, strep testing, and more.

 

Same-Day Clinic

Women's Health

The Womens Health Clinic provides routine gynecological exams, contraception consultations and prescriptions, and a full range of gynecological procedures.

Women's Health

LGBTQ+ Health

Campus Healths LGBTQ+ Health Clinic offers inclusive and comprehensive medical, mental health and transgender healthcare to folks of all gender identities.

LGBTQ+ Health

Nutrition

Certified dietitians offer a non-diet approach to nutrition and wellness through one-on-one nutrition appointments for a variety of concerns that need medical nutrition therapy.

Nutrition

Health Promotion

Provides outreach and education to give students the tools, skills, and information they need to make informed decisions about their health and wellness.

Health Promotion

View a comprehensive list of services offered at Campus Health.

Mental Health Support with Counseling & Psych Services (CAPS) at Campus Health

Counseling & Psych Services (CAPS)

Collaborative, individualized mental health support tailored to each student.

CAPS

Groups & Workshops

A variety of different options are available for students to learn useful mental health skills and connect with others in a safe space.

Groups & Workshops

Brief Individual Counseling & Psychiatry

CAPS is staffed with licensed mental health professionals, including counselors and psychiatrists, to support students through short-term, one-on-one services.

Brief Individual Counseling & Psychiatry

Survivor Support Services

Individualized support for survivors as they decide where to go after being subjected to violence.

Survivor Support Services

Specialty Services & Assessments

CAPS offers evaluation for ADHD, eating disorders, and substance use. Students can also search for providers by specialty area.

Specialty Services & Assessments

Self-Help Tools

Free everyday tools and resources curated by CAPS counselors to support student mental health.

Self-Help Tools

View a comprehensive list of services offered at CAPS.

CAMPUS REC

Fitness Classes at the Rec

Dance, martial arts, self-defense, yoga, Pilates, spinning, private yoga and Pilates classes and personal training. More than 50 classes each week.

Fitness Classes

Campus Recreation

Provides programs, services, educational opportunities and facilities that meet the recreational, fitness and wellness needs of students, faculty, staff, alumni and retirees.

Campus Rec Center

Arizona Intramurals

Mens, womens and co-ed teams play sports in a relaxed and friendly environment volleyball, flag football, soccer, softball, ultimate Frisbee and more.

UArizona Intramurals

Adaptive Athletics

The largest and most successful collegiate-based program in the country, the University of Arizona Adaptive Athletics Program houses seven competitive teams as well as an adaptive fitness center.

Adaptive Athletics
