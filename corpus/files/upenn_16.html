Penn Audit, Compliance and Privacy

Penn Audit, Compliance and Privacy

A Collective Responsibility to Protect Ourselves and Each Other from COVID-19

For information on how it works, answers to frequently asked questions, the privacy policy, and other resources, visit the PennOpen Pass website.

Our office provides services in three distinct yet related disciplines – audit, compliance, and privacy – in support of the vision to advance the University of Pennsylvania and Penn Medicine from excellence to eminence in the core endeavors of teaching, research, and service.

The Office of Audit, Compliance and Privacy functions in partnership with University and Penn Medicine management to anticipate and aggressively manage operational, financial, compliance, strategic and reputational risks; ensure the integrity of Penn’s systems of internal control; and ensure strong stewardship and management accountability at all levels.

On our website you can find information about our various services in the audit, compliance, and privacy arenas, meet the staff that provide these services, and locate a wide array of resources such as policies, guidance, best practice advisories and frequently asked questions relating to internal controls, information privacy and compliance. We invite you to browse our site and contact us with any questions or interests.

We hope we have provided the information you sought when coming to our virtual home; if not, please contact us.
