Women & Philanthropy

We are a community of mission-driven women who believe we can change the world through our collective philanthropy.

  • Join Now

Our members recognize that by giving together, their gift goes further and has greater influence. Each member commits an annual gift to support the Women & Philanthropy fund and is given opportunities throughout the year to meet faculty and students who benefit from their philanthropy. The strength of Women & Philanthropy is measured in its members when women give together, we can do more.

Our Mission

Women & Philanthropy at The Ohio State University connects generations of women who make a difference in the world by investing together in the people of Ohio State and their transformational work.
