Social Media Directory

Social Media Directory

Michigan State University

On the university-wide social media accounts, youll find the latest Michigan State University stories, news and highlights everything from Spartan pride to world-changing research. #SpartansWill

Admissions

Learn more about Michigan State University and its highly ranked academic programs, student organizations and important news and events on the Admissions social media accounts. Its good to #BeASpartan.

Alumni Office

The Michigan State University Alumni Office shares the collective impact of nearly half a million Spartans worldwide. Check out alumni stories and upcoming events on its accounts.

Athletics

From game updates to player profiles, get a behind-the-scenes look at all Michigan State University team sports on the Spartan Athletics social media accounts. #GoGreen

AgBioResearch

MSU AgBioResearch scientists conduct innovative research in the areas of food, energy and the environment.

 

Associated Students of Michigan State University (ASMSU)

Burgess Institute for Entrepreneurship & Innovation

The Burgess Institute for Entrepreneurship & Innovation empowers students to learn through action by providing programs, courses, and resources to foster an entrepreneurial mindset, develop empathy, and create new ventures.

Career Services Network

The Career Services Network (CSN) is a seamless connection of career service professionals located in college-based and centralized career centers across campus. We serve students and recent alumni (within two years of graduation.)

Center for Bioethics and Social Justice

Center for Survivors

The MSU Center for Survivors helps build empowered, inclusive communities while protecting and uplifting survivors through therapy, resources and other forms of support.

College of Agriculture and Natural Resources (CANR)

Faculty and students in the college are tackling the world’s biggest problems related to food, health and the environment.

College of Arts and Letters (CAL)

The college prepares students — from actors and filmmakers to philosophers and writers — to make their marks on their professions and the world.

College of Communication Arts and Sciences (ComArtSci)

Bringing together art and science, the college pioneers new ways for students to understand the world and use communication for the common good.

College of Education

The college’s top-ranked programs prepare students to be Spartan teachers and to lead as educators, researchers or health professionals.

College of Engineering

Spartan engineers are working to bring science and innovation into everyday life, with the college providing education, research and training areas from energy to materials to mobility.

College of Human Medicine

Committed to education, discovery, disseminating new knowledge and providing service at home and abroad, the college is nationally recognized as a pioneer in community-based medical education.

College of Law

The law college is preparing a talented and diverse community of students to be tomorrow’s activists, leaders and lawyers.

College of Music

World-class music and academic excellence come together in the music college, which serves as a destination for talented students and faculty from around the world. Learn about upcoming performances and more.

College of Natural Science

Providing outstanding educational opportunities, the college is home to 29 departments and programs in the biological, physical and mathematical sciences.

College of Nursing

The college works to advance the nursing profession and provide optimal health care, with a commitment to advocacy, education, research and best practices.

College of Osteopathic Medicine

One of the largest graduate medical education systems in the nation, with three locations in Michigan, the college is ranked highly among U.S. medical schools.

College of Social Science

Home to social, behavioral and economic sciences, the college encourages students to discover and pursue social issues, while solving complex problems.

College of Veterinary Medicine

In addition to offering internationally recognized programs in the areas of Doctor of Veterinary Medicine, the college provides exceptional clinical services and education, and shares expertise on human and animal health for a safer world.

Council of Graduate Students (COGS)

COGS is an authorized student government dedicated to the well-being and professional interests of graduate and professional students through advocacy, leadership and unity.

Department of Police and Public Safety

MSU police officers are sworn police officers certified by the State of Michigan who handle all police and criminal matters on campus. Learn about the services provided, as well as safety tips, parking matters and more.

Eat at State

Find complete information about dining on the MSU campus — locations, menus, hours of operation and more.

Eli and Edythe Broad Art Museum

MSUs Broad Art Museum is a dynamic contemporary art museum that serves as both a teaching institution and a cultural hub for East Lansing and the region. Follow events and programs at the Broad.

 

Eli Broad College of Business

One of the largest business schools in the country, the college develops transformational leaders who make business happen.

Facility for Rare Isotope Beams

FRIB is a scientific user facility that supports the mission of the Office of Nuclear Physics in the U.S. Department of Energy Office of Science. It will advance scientists’ understanding about the properties of rare isotopes and provide research opportunities for scholars from around the globe.

Gender and Sexuality Campus Center

The center leads and collaborates on university-wide initiatives that prepare students to thrive and provides services for students marginalized by their sexuality or gender identity.

Honors College

One of the country’s most distinctive and extensive honors programs, the college emphasizes individualized academic program planning. Learn about admission requirements, programs and initiatives.

Human Resources

HR provides information and resources about employment, benefits and more for Spartans @ Work.

Infrastructure Planning and Facilities (IPF)

IPF is responsible for the university’s physical environment. Follow for news and alerts about construction, green efforts and other projects.

Institutional Diversity and Inclusion

The office is focused on providing programming, education, training and funding that fosters an inclusive community at MSU.

James Madison College

In this policy-focused residential college, students study public affairs while developing strong communication, analytic and problem-solving skills.

Kellogg Hotel and Conference Center

In addition to being a go-to location for those visiting MSU, the Kellogg Center is a learning laboratory for students enrolled in the university’s School of Hospitality Business. Find info for dining, events and overnight stays.

Live On

Spartan life happens on campus. For information about campus residence options, MSU Neighborhoods and more, Live On.

Lyman Briggs College

Lyman Briggs College is the science-focused residential college at MSU. We study science in its diverse human, social, and global contexts, through small classes and a close-knit community.

MSU Dairy Store

Not only is it a sweet spot for the best ice cream in town, the Dairy Store is a plant, two retail spots, an educational and outreach center and more.

MSU Extension

By bringing the vast knowledge resources of MSU directly to individuals, communities and businesses throughout Michigan, MSU Extension makes a difference.

MSU Health Care

MSU Health Care is the fully-integrated academic medical center of Michigan State University, comprised of teaching faculty from the Colleges of Human Medicine, Osteopathic Medicine and Nursing.

MSU Libraries

Check out more than books! Discover a wealth of resources and events available to faculty, staff and students.

MSU Museum

Science and culture meet at this on-campus museum, which serves as a public steward for nearly a million objects and specimens in anthropology, natural science, folk arts and history from around the world.

MSU News

The official Twitter account for@michiganstateu news.

MSU Safe Place

MSU Safe Place provides free services and resources to Spartans and members of the surrounding community who need support because of relationship violence and/or stalking.

MSU Student Health and Wellness

On-campus, health-related services are provided to students to encourage personal health and promote a healthy learning environment.

MSU Tech Store

The store offers a range of retail products for both work and play, as well as tech assistance.

MSU Union

The MSU Union is a great place to study, eat and collaborate — and home to some of the biggest events on campus. Keep up with news, events and services.

MSUCOM Statewide Campus System

The Statewide Campus System is the Michigan State University College of Osteopathic Medicine educational consortium. The SCS exists to enhance the quality of postdoctoral education through the collective efforts of their member institutions.

Office for Education Abroad

The world is the classroom for Spartans. Learn about education abroad opportunities and destinations.

Office of the President

The official accounts for the Office of the President.

Recreational Sports and Fitness Services

Find information about sports, fitness and other recreational opportunities offered in multiple indoor and outdoor spaces on campus.

Residence Halls Association (RHA)

RHA is MSU’s on-campus student government. Learn how it’s improving the on-campus living experience and about upcoming events.

Residential College in the Arts and Humanities

RCAH is a residential college that inspires and prepares graduates to be collaborative learners and visionary change-makers through interdisciplinary study.

Resource Center for Persons with Disabilities (RCPD)

RCPD maximizes opportunities for those with disabilities, offering services, resources and advocacy that make MSU accessible and inclusive.

Shop MSU

Operated on the campus of Michigan State University, shop.msu.edu carries a variety of exclusive Spartan items. Proceeds from sales help benefit scholarships, programs, and departments.

Sparty

Sparty the Spartan is the university’s fearless and lovable mascot, a figure known throughout the state of Michigan and recognized across the nation.

Student Veterans Resource Center

The Student Veterans Resource Center is dedicated to promoting the educational, career, and personal advancement of service members, veterans, and their families at Michigan State University.

Sustainability

Unified in our goals to maximize the impact of our campus while minimizing our footprint, we use our collective knowledge, intuition, drive and passion to address sustainability challenges in everything we do.

Technology at MSU

TechAtMSU offers tech support, tips and solutions to help Spartans achieve their goals.

The Graduate School

The school serves as a hub of information for graduate education and beyond, enhancing the quality of experiences available to graduate students.

Undergraduate Education

The office oversees academic policies for MSU’s nearly 40,000 undergraduate students, and coordinates programs and initiatives to help students achieve success.

Undergraduate Research

For a growing number of Michigan State University students, undergraduate research is a defining experience in their college careers. Hands-on research makes them better learners and critical thinkers. It allows them to apply new knowledge to their studies and helps them map paths to graduation and beyond. MSU offers hundreds of individual and group-based research and creative activity opportunities.

University Archives and Historical Collections

A history as rich as MSU’s is a history worth preserving — and sharing. University Archives and Historical Collections provides records management services and maintains historical collections that support research and instruction.

University Licensing Programs

University Licensing promotes licensed products for the purposes of supporting MSU student scholarships and special programming and maintaining high standards for the brand.

University Outreach and Engagement

University Outreach and Engagement connects university-wide efforts to the greater community, increasing public access to university expertise and providing opportunities for over 32,000 students to engage in service learning each year.

University Procurement and Logistics

Delivering effective supply chain solutions to the Michigan State University campus community

Vice President for Research and Graduate Studies

The Office of Research & Innovation supports MSU’s research and creative activity by administering research funding, ensuring research integrity for both faculty and students, providing grants support services, fostering collaboration, licensing university inventions, and overseeing regulatory compliance. 

Wharton Center for Performing Arts

Wharton Center is where the performing arts come alive and live performance thrives. Find info about upcoming Broadway shows, concerts, speakers and more.

WKAR

WKAR is the public media outlet for MSU. Find news, exclusive content, updates and information from MSU’s public media channels.
