JLAMT

The Jewish Law Association of Middle Tennessee (JLAMT) is a fellowship group of professional lawyers and legal scholars serving the greater Nashville area and interested in exploring Jewish Law as well as the nexus between Jewish Law and American Law.

JLAMT is a certified Continuing Legal Education (CLE) provider under the auspices of the Tennessee State Bar Association. In addition to providing educational services, JLAMT provides advisory services on issues of Jewish Law, as well as facilitating alternate dispute resolution (ADR) services for interested parties by establishing connections with Jewish religious courts ("Batei Din", in Hebrew) both locally and nationally, that can function as binding arbitrators.
