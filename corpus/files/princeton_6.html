Connecting Industry Across Campus

Princetons Strategic Partnership and Engagement professionals work with companies to develop collaborations that accelerate innovation and are mutually beneficial. The corporate engagement team facilitates relationships that promote the educational, scientific and scholarly mission of the University.  The team helps companies explore the considerable engagement opportunities with faculty and students at Princeton.
