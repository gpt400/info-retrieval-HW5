Read more about this expert

Georgia Tech is at the forefront of cutting-edge research, spanning fields including engineering, computing, business, health, cybersecurity, and public policy.

Our research professionals welcome opportunities to share their expert analysis and perspectives on the latest news and trends, and you're in the right place to connect with them. Each photo on this page links to information such as faculty profiles, and social media and research websites highlighting their expertise.

Submit requests to connect with them by emailing media@gatech.edu or by clicking the Get in touch with this expert button, found on each experts page. Be sure to include your story deadline, and look for a response from the Media Relations team within 24 hours.

Alex Hsu

Scheller College of Business

David Joyner

David Joyner, an accomplished educator and technology enthusiast, excels in advancing online learning while leveraging artificial intelligence and innovative strategies.

Srijan Kumar

Jennifer Leavey

College of Sciences School of Biological Sciences
