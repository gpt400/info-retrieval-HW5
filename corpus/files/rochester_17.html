In Pursuit of Meliora

At the University of Rochester's Warner School of Education and Human Development, 
the spirit of Meliora informs all that we do. It's our motto and the heart of our mission:
To make the world ever better. 

Here, leaders learn to innovate and collaborate in their fields, combining research, scholarship and practice to make a meaningful difference in schools and communities everywhere.
