Give to the College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences

Your gift strengthens our focus on efficiency, economic viability, environmental compatibility and social responsibility.

Featured Fund

The Ohio State Fund for the College of Food, Agricultural and Environmental Sciences
Give now

Make your impact

The College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences is committed to providing programs that respond to the complex needs of Ohios population and the demands on the states resources. Integrated learning enables students to work together to address environmental problems and societal needs.

The Ohio State Fund for the College of Food, Agricultural and Environmental Sciences

Provides discretionary monies for activities such as outreach, alumni relations, students' co-curricular activities and undergraduate research.

Waterman Facilities Fund

Used for facilities projects at Waterman including but not limited to design, construction, maintenance, and/or renovations.

The CFAES Student Success Fund

Supports initiatives and staffing, in the College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences around Student Success, including but not limited to K-12 programming, student experience, leadership development, certification, and training programs.

The CFAES Translating Research Fund

Supports initiatives, research, support, and staffing around translating research in CFAES

Ohio 4-H Program Fund

Supplies, Services and Program Support

The Fund for Ohio State Extension

Discretionary support for Ohio State University Extension including but not limited to staffing, research, or programming that strengthens families and communities, prepares youth for success, enhances agriculture and the environment, and advances employment and income opportunities.

Waterman Multi-Species Facility Fund

Designated to CFAES; supports the planning, construction and operations of a multi-species facility at Waterman.

Waterman Controlled Environment Agriculture Research Complex Fund

Supports construction and maintenance of the Controlled Environment Agriculture Research Complex at Waterman.

Chadwick Arboretum & Learning Gardens Annual Fund

Supports the arboretum's mission of providing an extensive and diverse plant collection for education, research, and enjoyment

Students First, Students Now Scholarship Fund in FAES

Provides need-based scholarship monies for students in the College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences

Agriculture Alumni Society Scholarship

Provides scholarship support to students enrolled in the College of Agriculture.

College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences Student Support Fund

Provides funds to support CFAES students financially, emotionally, or academically on a case-by-case basis.

CFAES Nourishing Success Scholarship Fund

Funds are to be used to support scholarships to cover the costs of a basic university dining plan for junior or senior student(s) who are enrolled in the College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences, live off campus, and demonstrate financial need. Scholarships are renewable as long as recipients meet the selection criteria. Recipients will be selected by the college's scholarship committee.

Agricultural Technical Institute Scholarship Fund

General Scholarships

CFAES Study Abroad Fund

The annual distribution from the fund shall be used to provide study abroad scholarship support for undergraduate students in the College of Food, Agricultural, and Environmental Sciences who are participating in study abroad programs.
